Our Story

I made this shoe online store for basketball shoes, since I'm a sneakerhead
myself and basketball shoes have one of the best designs when it comes to
shoes. Many of the NBA players have their own basketball shoes since they
have a contract with different shoe companies.

The target group for this campaign is for people who are interested in
basketball shoes, as they are the most popular right now in the world and want
to the people who wants to show some style both on and off the court. I get
some inspiration from other shoe stores such as Nike, Foot-locker, Adidas etc.
